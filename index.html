Pantorī: A search engine for the kitchen.

A (work in progress) search engine for recipes.

Input any aspect of a type of recipe that you are looking for, whether it be the name of the chef, an ingredient, method, or whatever and click search to see any recipes that may match. Keep in mind this is a constantly growing list of recipes that I will be adding to as time goes on.
